# If you have discovered a security vulnerability in b1n0, please
# report it to security@b1n0.com. We commit to acknowledging your
# report within 5 business days. Do not publicly disclose the issue
# until we have had a reasonable opportunity to remediate.

# Out of scope:
# - Social engineering of Tres33 employees or contractors
# - Physical attacks against Tres33 infrastructure
# - Denial-of-service attacks
# - Findings from automated scanners without demonstrable impact
